Storm-Tracking

(the perfect complement to RADAR weather data)

This system allows, in real-time monitoring the evolution and its probable trajectory

of a weather phenomenon, anticipating the event with a high level of probability.

By means of Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools and the analysis of the data and RADAR

images, the system determines in real time the probability of occurrence of the

storm over the selected areas. One of the principal characteristics of the system is

the issuing early warnings in automatic way to the monitoring control center

for prevention of the potential catastrophe.
